Hello! My Name is Jaime Aaron Valmores Acuña III

I was born and raised in an island called Bohol on the central part of the Philippines. I was one of the first Filipino missionaries to be assigned to serve on the Tonga Nuku'alofa Mission. Now I am studying in an university located on the Ouahu Island, which means, so far in my life, I have been living on islands.

A more personal detail about me is that I am a huge fan of boxing. I grew up playing basketball with my dad, siblings, and friends as it is the most popular sport anywhere in the Philippines. It was not until I got home from my mission that I was able to finally have access to boxing and was immediately addicted to it.

These are the figures of boxing that has inspired me the most:

  1. Vasiliy Lomachenko
  2. Mike Tyson
  3. Mark Magsayo
