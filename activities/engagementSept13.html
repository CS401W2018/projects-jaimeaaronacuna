What are special entity characters?

These are characters we want to display to the user but have extra steps to be taken as these characters could be misunderstood by HTML. These characters are presented by &"name";.

What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

OL stands ordered list, this tag is used when the order of the lists matters.
UL stands for unordered list, this tag us used when were just listing things without consideration of its order.
