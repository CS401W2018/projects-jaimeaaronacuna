About Chocolate Hills

Chocolate Hills is one of the icon of the province. Tens of "perfect-shaped" hills across two towns of the island. These Hills are featured in the 200 peso bill of the country.

Chocolate Hills got their name from their appearance. During summer season, the vegitation covering these Hills turn brown making them look like Hershey's Chocolate Kisses.
