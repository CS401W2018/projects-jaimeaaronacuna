About Panglao

Panglao Holds the world famous beaches of Bohol. Every year, more than a million tourist visits this town of the Island.

There are many attractions in this town alone. From all the different beaches on its coasts, an underground cave pool, islands that can be visited via small boats, and a lot of resorts and restaurants in the area.
