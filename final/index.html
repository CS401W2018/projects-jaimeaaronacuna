Bohol is one of the most fun island in the Philippines. There are a lot of activities to do, places to see, and events to take part in. This page will be your guide in making the most of your time here.

About Bohol

An Island Province of the Philippines. Bohol is one of the most sought after destination of the nation. The inhabitants of this island speaks the Bisaya language and most are also able to communicate in Tagalog and even in English.

Considered as one of the most popular and most tourist-friendly destinations in the Philippines. Attracting visitors with its stunning and diverse attractions.
