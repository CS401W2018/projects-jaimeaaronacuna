My Interests: Combat Sports

Boxing

A type of combat sport that is strickly faught with fists. Although there are many aspects to this sport, a boxing match's offensive side is purely of punches which means there's no slapping, grabbing, backhands, elbows, kicks, and knees. This aspect has made sport focus on the science of one aspect of striking which in turn made it very entertaining to watch.

Mixed Martial Arts (MMA)

The most complete of any combat sports in terms of variations on fighting styles. This sport not only even goes beyond striking and includes grappling which drastically changes the way fights are faught as there are new angles of attack in the offensive and there are threats of takedowns on the defensive. Although the sport seems very dangerous in nature, MMA is significantly safer than boxing due to the nature of the rules of the sport.

Muay Thai

Also sometimes referred to as Thai Boxing. This combat sport is complete in all aspects of striking but does not allow any grappling (except clinches) which translate to an exciting striking spectacle. The discipline is also known as "The Art of Eight Limbs" as Muay Thai utilizes two hands, two elbows, two knees, and two feet as its weaponry.
